Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25-July-2025

Last Updated: 06-August-2025

EcoPantryPal ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information: Name and email address (used during login or registration)

b. Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app usage statistics

c. Receipt and Product Data: Scanned receipt images and extracted purchase details, barcode data

d. Pantry Data: Items you add to your virtual pantry

e. Carbon Emission Data: Calculated impact based on food items

f. Push Notifications: For upcoming expiration reminders

2. Tracking & App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

We may collect and link certain data (such as name, email, or usage activity) with third-party services to measure performance or improve features. This may be considered "tracking" under Apple’s AppTrackingTransparency framework.

Before any tracking occurs, we will request your explicit permission via the iOS AppTrackingTransparency prompt. You can choose to allow or deny tracking at any time in your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable core app features (receipt scanning, pantry tracking)
  • Calculate and show carbon impact
  • Send expiration alerts
  • Improve app performance
  • Customer support
  • Analytics (with your ATT consent)

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with third-party services such as OCR, barcode scanning, notifications, and analytics. These services process data according to their own privacy policies and, where applicable, after you have given tracking consent through ATT.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain data only as long as needed and apply encryption and access control to safeguard it.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data, and opt out of push notifications at any time via device settings. You may also revoke tracking permission in iOS settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.

7. Children’s Privacy

EcoPantryPal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app or on our website.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact us at: founder@ecopantrypal.com

10. Data Sharing & Tracking

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with service providers who help us operate the app and deliver its features. Any data used for tracking is processed only after you have granted permission via the AppTrackingTransparency prompt.
